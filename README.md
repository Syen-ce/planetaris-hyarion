# Welcome to Hyarion

A planet made from crystals and lava, light refraction rays fall from the sky and destroys the surface.

#Features
*   1 new planet, Hyarion
*   5 new machines
*   A better assembling machine
*   2 new science packs
*   +10 new technologies

![hyarion generation](https://i.imgur.com/Qb85yTO.jpeg)

# Is it safe to add the mod to an existing save?
At this point is completely safe to play in an existing save, it doesn't alter any base recipes

# Inside Hyarion

**(This section contains spoilers)**

### Polishing

Polish raw gemstones to produce new and more dangerous ones, you will need the polisher and polishing compound. Gems are mined from geodes found all over hyarion using a new mining drill.

![basic polishing mechanic show-off](https://i.imgur.com/i8OXUHp.jpeg)

### Space facilities

Space facilites introduce more space dependent production and items to expand your spaceships and create your interplanetary travelling factory!

![show-off of space facilities production](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Syen-ce/planetaris-factorio-images/refs/heads/main/space-facilities.png)

# Planetaris unbounded

Planetaris unbounded is a bigger mod that merges all planetaris planets, Hyarion is the second planet of the development and depends on the planet Arig to work.

![spagetti lines of hyarion production](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Syen-ce/planetaris-factorio-images/refs/heads/main/hyarion-prod.png)
