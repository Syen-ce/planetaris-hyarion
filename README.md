# Welcome to Hyarion

A planet made from crystals and lava, light refraction rays fall from the sky and destroys the surface. Polish gemstones and research refraction to discover new materials. You can visit Hyarion after completing Fulgora.

![hyarion generation](https://github.com/Syen-ce/planetaris-factorio-images/blob/main/hyarion-1.png)

# Inside Hyarion

**(This section contains spoilers)**

### Polishing

Polish raw gemstones to produce new and more dangerous ones, you will need the polisher and polishing compound. Gems are mined from geodes found all over hyarion using a new mining drill.

![basic polishing mechanic show-off](https://github.com/Syen-ce/planetaris-factorio-images/blob/main/hyarion-polishing.png)

### Refraction

Experiment with more rare gemstones and light to create new unstable materials and generate energy.

![basic polishing mechanic show-off](https://github.com/Syen-ce/planetaris-factorio-images/blob/main/hyarion-refraction.png)

### Space facilities

Space facilites introduce more space dependent production and items to expand your spaceships and create your interplanetary travelling factory!

![show-off of space facilities production](https://github.com/Syen-ce/planetaris-factorio-images/blob/main/hyarion-3.png)
